How To Become a Vendor

Before becoming a vendor on promptearn, you need to first of all sign up as an affiliate. The reason for this is to give you access to our marketplace; then on the marketplace you can decide to list your product automatically.

Take Note: You need to understand the TERMS AND CONDITIONS concerning being a vendor on our platform.

Once you have read the terms and conditions and you are satisfied with it, the next thing is to register and list the product directly on our marketplace.
