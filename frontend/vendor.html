How to list your products on PromptEarn marketplace as a vendor

To continuously uphold the quality of products listed on our platform, there are certain requirements that every PromptEarn vendor needs to meet before listing their products on our marketplace.

You need to read and understand PromptEarn’s terms and conditions for vendors. Click here to read our terms and conditions.

After reading the “terms and conditions” and you are satisfied with it, you can then proceed to send an email containing the details of your products to vendor@promptearn.com for approval

After submitting your product, we will conduct a quality check and list it on our website within 24 to 96 hours if it meets our standards. The decision is typically communicated to vendors via the email they provided during the product submission process.




What are the details needed to list your product?

Name of vendor
Name of product
Sales page link :- We would need a link to your product sales page, this is compulsory. If you don’t have a sales page, please hire a good copywriter to write one for you(if you’d rather have one of our in -house copywriters work on your sales page, send an email to hire@promptearn.com )
Product delivery process (access link) :- This is required so that we can verify the product quality and ensure it’s simple and easy to access for buyers.
Price of the product
Commission offers :- The percentage you intend to pay affiliates for each unit sold. Please note that affiliates go for products that benefits them the most. So the higher the percentage, the higher the number of sales and affiliates the product would get
Customer support information:- Your customer support information where buyers of your product can easily reach someone to help them if they have any issue wih your product
Promotional materials (if any)
Link to sales challenge page (if any)
Vendor’s personal email address
Please note that it takes 2-5 working days after submissions to get an approval. This is because we'd have to verify and do quality checks on your products to decide if it meets our standard

For Registered Vendors

You can always list new products as many times as you want. However, note that the 2-5 working days quality check is applicable each time.





Payment Policy

Kindly note that PromptEarn automatically collects and splits sales payments amongst you, the affiliates and PromptEarn platform.

PromptEarn charges 10% of the product cost. This means that for example, if a product cost ₦30,000, PromptEarn charges ₦3,000 transaction for each sale.

You can withdraw your earnings from PromptEarn wallet every saturday and get credited on your local bank account every sunday
